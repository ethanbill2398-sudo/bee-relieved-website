What is Manual Osteopathic Therapy?

Thorough Initial Assessment
Your first visit includes an in-depth discussion of your health history, and a detailed assessment of your posture and physical function to further understand your body.
Osteoarticular Adjustments
A gentle way to improve joint movement and position using light muscle contractions instead of high force thrusts, with no cracking involved.
Myofascial Release
Slow, sustained stretching of soft tissues to release tension and improve movement — the part of a massage without oil.
Craniosacral Therapy
Subtle techniques that release restrictions within the cranial bones(skull), sacrum, and spinal cord to support your nervous system.
Visceral Manipulation
Uses gentle myofascial techniques to restore the natural movement and function of your internal organs. Used to support the digestive, reputation, urinary and reproductive systems!
Lymphatic Drainage
Encourages healthy lymphatic flow to help reduce swelling and support your body’s natural detox process.
